About Quran Journal

Application Concept:
Quran Journal is a simple desktop application that helps Muslim users read and reflect on Surah from the Al-Quran.

Purpose:
This application allows users to explore Surah details such as Surah name, English Translation, number of ayahs, and revelation type retrieved from the Al-Quran Cloud API The app encourages users to record their spiritaul reflections and track reading progress for self-improvement.

"Do they bot then reflect on the Qur'an?"
- Surah Muhammad (47:24)

Target Users:
Muslim individuals, student is Islamic studies, and those who wish to deepen their understanding of through daily journaling and reflection.

Usage Context:
Quran Journal can be used at home, in religious classes, or by students in tahfiz institutions to document notes and understanding for each surah.
